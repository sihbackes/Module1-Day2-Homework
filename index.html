Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your tates buds with festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart
tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes(scrape rind off with a paring knife desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
